Product Details

Perfect for those cool summer nights, our newest Crasche hat provides minimal insulation, while offering all the protection of our winter variety.
For extreme sports, and our most secure fit, see Thrashe below.
Size: Size Med/Large - 21.5" to 24" circumference. Size XL - 24"+ circumference. For a size small, see the Thrashe Hat below. Materials: This hat is comprised of super light weight cotton-like acrylic fabric. The outer layer of the inserts is composed of an extremely strong polycarbonate plastic, the same material used to make bullet-proof glass. The inner layers are composed of neoprene rubber, the same skin-friendly material used in making wet suits. The inner layer of neoprene rubber has a series of air chambers. These air chambers help cushion the impact, and help spread the force of the impact over the entire insert. Cleaning: Machine wash, air dry, or low dry. Take the inserts out prior to washing.
